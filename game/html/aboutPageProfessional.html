ClickCon

ClickCon originally began as an experiment for a board game, just to learn how to set up proximity type things and learn better structures for data and maps. However, its simplistic nature quickly took the main spotlight, and with some positive feedback from friends and others whom it was showed to, it took off into its own epic, full project.

That project began on August 28th, 2017, and look where it is now. To see the original, less evolved version, click here

The basis of ClickCon is simplicity. The complexity comes from the variety of strategies involved, where the focus is more on strategy and understanding than motor skills and coordination.

The rules are simple enough, click a planet once to capture it as your own, as long as one you already own is connected. Whoever has the most points at the end of the turn limit wins. How one achieves that is where the fun truly begins...
